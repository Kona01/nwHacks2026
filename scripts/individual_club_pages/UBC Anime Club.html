UBC Anime Club

Welcome to UBC Anime Club! We are a social club that brings together students who share a passion for anime, manga, and Japanese culture. Every Friday we host free, drop-in weekly events on campus, featuring artist meet-ups, anime screenings, and a different main activity each week! These include but are not limited to:

  • Karaoke Nights
  • Cosplay Workshops
  • Board Game Nights
  • Dance Workshops

We also run larger, seasonal events, such as holiday-themed socials and our one-day annual convention Pop-Up Hanami, as well as outings and collaborations with other clubs.

While we are an 'anime' club, we house a broad community who also have interests in adjacent areas like:

  • rhythm and video games;
  • fashion and cosplay;
  • seiyuu and vocaloid;
  • light novels, manhwa, manhua, and webtoons;
  • art, music, and photography;
  • generally anything you'd geek out about!

So come hang and chill with usyou don't even have to watch anime ;)

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I join?

A: Come to our free weeklies, happening every Friday usually in Buchanan! No need to sign up! More information on the weekly event can be found in our recent posts on Instagram or Discord.

Q: How much are memberships?

A: Annual memberships are $10.00 for UBC Students. Have a membership card from previous years? Returning members are eligible for a discount!

Q: What do I get out of a membership?

A: We are a non-profit club that relies on you to allow us to keep running our free weekly events! UBCAni members receive the following benefits:

  • Discounts of 10-20% at our sponsor locations across the lower mainland;
  • Discounts on our ticketed events and merch;
  • Perks at our events and weeklies (like free snacks);
  • A shiny new membership card design each year.
