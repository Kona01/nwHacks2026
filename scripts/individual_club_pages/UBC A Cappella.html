UBC A Cappella

ABOUT US

UBC A Cappella is located on the traditional, ancestral, and unceded land of the Skwxwú7mesh (Squamish), Stó:lō and Səl̓ílwətaʔ/Selilwitulh (Tsleil-Waututh), and xwməθkwəy̓əm (Musqueam) Nations.

Our story started in 2007: the UBC A Cappella Club was formed, comprising of one group called "The Undeclared Majors." 18 years later, our club has grown to include four auditioned ensembles, one non-auditioned ensemble, and two competitive ICCA groups.

We are proud to be the largest Collegiate A Cappella collective in Canada.

A close-knit family of singers, songwriters, and vocal percussionists, our rehearsals are full of singing, collaborating, laughing, and bonding with students from all faculties. Our repertoire is representative of the diverse musical influences brought to the group by our members, and we look forward to sharing it with you!

OUR GROUPS

UBC A Cappella is home to 7 different vocal ensembles, divided into 2 streams.

Competitive

Our groups have had immense success on the International and National Competitive A Cappella stages. From soothing harmonies to explosive choreography, our groups bring the best every year!

Eh? Cappella

Eh? Cappella is one of UBC A Cappella’s competitive ICCA groups. They are 7x ICCA semifinalists, and their 4th place 2024 ICCA West Semifinals set is available on Spotify! They are known for their dynamic arrangements, captivating stage presence, and of course, showstopping vocals. Eh? Cappella seamlessly blends genres such as pop, R&B, and rock, to create truly unique and exciting pieces that cannot be found anywhere else!

Musical Directors: Giuliana Kim & Ryota Misaki

Group Size: 18 Members

Rehearsals: Mondays from 6:00 - 9:00pm, and Saturdays from 11:00am - 2:00pm.

 

The Northwest Collective

The Northwest Collective (NWC) is known for their tangible sense of connection on stage, which has earned them awards at the last four ICCA quarterfinals. Their sets always include complex arrangements, exciting choreography, and engaging stage presence. You can stream their 2025 EP "Forever" on Spotify and other streaming platforms!

Musical Directors: Michelle Emond & Kira Potter

Group Size: 16 Members

Rehearsals: Thursdays from 6:00 - 9:00pm, and Sundays from 12:00 - 3:00pm.

 

General

Our groups have performed for local charities, hospitals, and even at the President of UBC's home! As non-competitive groups, their work culminates in our two term-end concerts.

The Undeclared Majors

The Undeclared Majors is the oldest a cappella group at UBC and the only general group holding two weekly rehearsals. This extra rehearsal time means that this particular group has a lot more time to bond as a full group as they place a lot of importance on the social aspect of aca! Their setlist this coming year will include some recent popular music you know and love, but their overall group sound also leans towards R&B, jazz, and oldies!

Musical Directors: Francheska Magbanua & Eva Wang

Group Size: 26-30 Members

Rehearsals: Tuesdays and Thursdays at 6:00 - 8:00pm.

 

The Unaccompanied Minors

The second group to form in UBC A Cappella, The Unaccompanied Minors are known for their experimental arrangements across an array of genres, tight vocal cohesion as a result of their ride-or-die bond, and red accents! They lean more towards a chill, R&B, vibey style, but love to throw in some pop too!

Musical Directors: Hana Akira Domingo & Jalen Reyes

Group Size: 20-25 Members

Rehearsals: Thursdays at 7:00 - 9:30pm.

 

Fermata UBC

Fermata is a vibrant and positive group for gender-diverse singers, including but not limited to women, genderqueer people, genderfluid people, and non-binary people! Their setlists are often upbeat and pop-forward, though they typically balance the vibe with some slower contemporary music.

Musical Directors: Lola Lin & Sarah Tyler

Group Size: 28-35 Members

Rehearsals: Mondays at 6:30 - 9:00pm.

 

Choral Reef

UBC A Cappella's largest auditioned group is Choral Reef. Known for their playful choreography and full sound, you can distinguish them by their playful performances and blue accents! Like a real reef, each member is celebrated for their uniqueness while forming one vibrant and welcoming ecosystem. Dive into their full sound!

Musical Directors: Maria Loo Cordoba & Zeynep Sugle

Group Size: 35-45 Members

Rehearsals: Wednesdays at 6:30 - 9:00 pm.

 

Fast & Forte

“There’s always room for Family.” The largest group in UBC A Cappella, Fast & Forte is a non-auditioned group appropriate for singers of all skill levels. Known for their gold accents and tight-knit bonds, this group is family embodied!

Musical Directors: Sumay Baidya, Josh Dar Santos, & Paul Misa

Group Size: 60+ Members

Rehearsals: Wednesdays at 6:00 - 8:00pm.
